Research Overview

My research lies at the intersection of Blockchain Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity. I study how large language models (LLMs) and intelligent systems can support secure, explainable, and efficient smart-contract development and auditing.

As a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, I work under the supervision of Dr. Chris Brown as part of the ChainSentinel Projectâ€” a multi-agent LLM framework for smart-contract auditing and verification.

Future Directions
